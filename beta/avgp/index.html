A Very Generic Platformer - AVGP

AVGP is a Wii Homebrew platformer game where you move a cube across increasingly difficult levels... while it just makes fun of you.

It's deliberately designed to be passive aggressive. think I Wanna Be The Guy except it's not difficult... not as difficult, anyway.

This is a very early beta, so there's a lot missing from the concept.

To play this, you need either a Wii, Wii U, or Dolphin. Or something else that can run Wii homebrew, i dont know
